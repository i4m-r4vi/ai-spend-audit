# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy: AI Spend Audit

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Founders, CTOs, and Engineering Managers at startups (10-200 employees).
- **Secondary:** Freelance agencies and consultants looking to optimize their software margins.

## Value Proposition
"Companies are wasting thousands of dollars annually on redundant AI tool subscriptions. Our free audit analyzes your stack and recommends optimal configurations so you can slash costs without losing productivity."

## Acquisition Channels
1. **Product Hunt Launch:**
   - Launch Day target: Top 3 Product of the Day.
   - Strategy: Emphasize "Free Audit" and "Instant ROI".
2. **Twitter/X & LinkedIn:**
   - Share anonymous case studies of savings (e.g., "How a 15-person dev team saved $4k/yr by switching from Copilot + ChatGPT to Cursor").
3. **Virality & Word of Mouth:**
   - The shareable URL generated at the end of the audit acts as a viral loop. Users share their "Savings Score" with peers.

## Economics (Monetization Strategy)
- **Current MVP:** Free lead generation.
- **Future Upsell:** Introduce premium "Enterprise Audits" ($499 one-time) for massive orgs requiring compliance reviews, or take an affiliate cut from recommended tools (e.g., Cursor referral links).
