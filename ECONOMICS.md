# Economics & Monetization

## Cost Structure
- **Frontend Hosting:** Vercel (Free Tier initially, then $20/mo Pro).
- **Backend Hosting:** Render (Free Tier initially, then $7/mo Starter).
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas (Free Tier initially, then Serverless).
- **AI Processing:** Anthropic API (Pay-as-you-go). Estimated at ~$0.01 per summary generated.
- **Email Delivery:** Resend ($0 on free tier up to 3k emails).

## Monetization Plan
Currently, the tool operates as a **Lead Generation Magnet** for our core TechVruk consulting business.

**Step 1: Free Audit (Current)**
Provide immediate value. Capture email if savings > $500/mo.

**Step 2: Affiliate Revenue (Mid-term)**
Insert referral links for recommended tools (e.g., Cursor, Windsurf) in the final report.

**Step 3: Enterprise AI Compliance Audits (Long-term)**
Sell a $999/mo ongoing monitoring service that hooks directly into a company's SSO/Okta to detect shadow IT and unused AI subscriptions automatically.
