# Key Performance Indicators (Metrics)

To evaluate the success of the AI Spend Audit SaaS, we will track the following metrics:

## Acquisition
- **Unique Pageviews:** Target > 5,000 in week 1 (Product Hunt launch).
- **Bounce Rate:** Target < 40%. The hero section must clearly communicate value immediately.

## Activation
- **Form Completion Rate:** Percentage of users who start the audit vs complete it. Target > 60%. (Monitored via PostHog funnel analytics).
- **Average Time to Complete:** Target < 90 seconds.

## Retention & Viral Loop
- **Share Rate:** Percentage of users who copy their `/share/:id` link. Target > 15%.
- **K-Factor (Virality):** How many new users does one shared link generate? Target > 0.2.

## Revenue / Lead Gen
- **Lead Capture Rate:** Percentage of users saving >$500 who book a consultation. Target > 5%.
- **Cost per Lead (CPL):** Driven purely by API costs (Anthropic). Estimated at < $0.05 per qualified lead.
