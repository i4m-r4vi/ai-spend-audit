# User Interviews

## Pre-Development Validation

**Interviewee 1: Sarah, CTO at a 40-person startup**
- *Pain Point:* "We bought Copilot for all devs, but half of them also expensed ChatGPT Plus on their corporate cards. I don't know who is using what, and I suspect we're double-paying."
- *Feedback on Solution:* "If a tool could just tell me exactly what to consolidate based on their roles, I'd use it instantly."
- *Takeaway:* Validates the "Coding Stack Optimization" rule.

**Interviewee 2: Marcus, Agency Owner (15 employees)**
- *Pain Point:* "Everyone is using Claude for writing copy, but we also have a legacy ChatGPT Team plan that no one cancelled."
- *Feedback on Solution:* "I don't have time to do the math. Just give me a clear dollar amount of what I'm wasting."
- *Takeaway:* Emphasize the "Total Monthly Savings" in massive text on the Results Dashboard.

**Interviewee 3: Emily, Freelancer**
- *Pain Point:* "I pay for Midjourney, ChatGPT, and Claude. It's like $70/mo."
- *Feedback on Solution:* "As a solo worker, the savings aren't massive enough to warrant booking a consultation with you, but I'd like a newsletter."
- *Takeaway:* Add a conditional CTA. Only push for a consultation if savings > $500/mo. Otherwise, capture the email for a newsletter.
